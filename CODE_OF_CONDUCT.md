# Code of Conduct

TWDxHouseKeeping is a small, focused open-source project. We welcome contributors who share our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

## Our Values

**Respect** — Treat every contributor as a capable professional. Assume good intent and engage with ideas on their merits.

**Clarity** — Communicate directly and honestly. Raise concerns early, explain your reasoning, and invite others to do the same.

**Care** — Take pride in the work. Review thoroughly, test carefully, and leave things better than you found them.

**Collaboration** — Share knowledge freely. A question asked is an opportunity to improve documentation; a suggestion made is a chance to make the project better for everyone.

## In Practice

- Give feedback on code, not on people.
- When you disagree, say so clearly and explain why — then listen.
- Credit others' contributions.
- If something feels off, raise it with a maintainer.

## Scope

This conduct applies in all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication made in the context of this project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation when these values are not upheld. Concerns can be raised privately by opening a blank issue titled "Code of Conduct" — maintainers will reach out directly.
